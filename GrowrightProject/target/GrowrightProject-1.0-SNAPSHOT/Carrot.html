CARROTS

What is a Carrot?

A carrot is a nutritious, root vegetable that comes in varies colours such as white, yellow, purple and most commonly known in orange. It is a biennial plant that is part of the Apiaceae family that produces taproots that are edible. Carrots are originally from Turkey, Iran and Afghanistan. It can be consumed raw or when processed.

Steps To Planting Carrots

Steps:

  • Carrots need to be panted deep (20 cm) and it needs to be 30 cm wide.
  • The container must have drainage holes at the bottom.
  • To catch the water droplets, put a saucer under the container.
  • Fill the container with potting soil mix until there is 2.5 cm between the soil and the top of the container.
  • It is beneficial to use potting soil mix than using the soil in your garden as the potting soil mix has the right nutrients to grow your carrots.
  • The Potting soil mix must have 2 pH between 6.0 and 6.8.
  • Carrots prefer light, sandy and loamy weather. Carrots need cool tempereture between 10''C and 18''C to thrive.
  • They need atleast 6 hours of sunlight per day. place the carrots close to the window for 2-3 months until harvest. Altanetively, you can get grow lights.
  • Add water into the potting soil mix, sprinkle carrot seeds on top of the soil and then sprinkle a little bit of water on top.
  • Carrots will need 14-17 days to sprout and the top layer of the soil needs to be moist at all times. When the carrots have sprouted, thin out the carrot sprout using scissors son that each has 1.3cm space on all sides.
  • Repeat the thinning process when the seedlings are 7.6cm
  • Carrots take about 60-75 days to mature so when they have matured you can harvest and eat them right away.
  • It is advised to plant your container in intervals to prevent harvesting too many carrots all at the same time.

    Storage

    Carrots become very dry when they have been peeled, this is because the peels act as a protective layer. To store peeled carrots properly:

  • If stored properly, carrots can stay fresh for up to 2-3 weeks in the fridge.
  • Make sure to wrap the unpeeled and unwashed carrots in a dry paper towel and place them in an airtight container or bag.
  • Store the container in the coolest part of the fridge.
  • Wash the peeled carrots thoroughly.
  • In a container, add a bit of cool water and place the carrots inside and place that container in the fridge.
  • Make sure to change the water evey few days.
  • Rinse the carrots before eating them.

    • Carrot Benefits

    Carrots are one of the most nutritious vegetable. They are a good source of vitamin K, potassium and antioxidants, when included in a diet. Carrots are known to potentially prevent cardiovascular disease and some types of cancers. Carrots help to balance blood sugar levels. They improve vision and immune system.
